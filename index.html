About Our Club

Founded in the heart of Karingal, Victoria, The Karingal Computer Club Inc. is a non-profit social organization dedicated to bringing together local technology enthusiasts. Whether you are a seasoned sysadmin, a retro computing hobbyist, or someone just looking to learn how to build your first PC, our doors are open.

Our purpose is to foster knowledge sharing, provide a supportive environment for troubleshooting and learning, and build genuine community connections through our shared passion for technology. We host regular meetups, hardware swap meets, and collaborative workshops.
